Welcome!

Archival status

This site has been archived until further notice. It is a static copy of the site available on August 22, 2017, with slight changes to remove elements that would not function properly on a static site. We intend to revert to the previously-running application in the future. In the meantime, we may run auctions via the listserv with email responses, and intend to resume posting opportunities to the site after reverting. Email us at micropurchase@gsa.gov with any questions.

The Micro-purchase Marketplace is the place to bid on open-source issues from the 18F team. Learn more about this experiment

Be sure to register your company before placing a bid. If you have any questions, please email us at micropurchase@gsa.gov.
